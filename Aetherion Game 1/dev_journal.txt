## ARCHITECTURE NOTES
- **Environment as Narrative:** The Caeloria map is structured to reflect cognitive distortion (e.g., mismatched road segments, asymmetrical village core).
- **Modular Design:** Systems should be decoupled (e.g., Dialogue system should not depend directly on Player script).
- **Lore-First:** All item descriptions, NPC names, and location data must pull from or be validated against `Aetherion.txt`.

## LORE IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
- **Reality Anchor:** Marcellus serves as the outsider perspective, confirming that the cognitive distortions in Caeloria (Elias and Septimus) are localized and supernatural anomalies, not standard world behavior.
- **Dual Memory Distortion:** Civilian identity decay (Elias) paired with military structural corruption (Septimus) established in Caeloria region.
- **Timeline:** The game is set in 5260 AW during the Vallian Succession Crisis.
- **Geography:** Initial region focus likely to be Vallia (Aurelia).
- **Races:** Ensure proper handling of Elven subtypes (High, Wood, Coastal) and Human lineages.
